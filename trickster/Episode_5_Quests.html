Episode 5
Rebirth of Memories
Through the Memory Cell Regenerating Device on the dev room's Mother Computer, adventures are able to get a glimpse into the past of Don Giuvanni, Rosaline, and Don Cavalier, who all hold the keys to the secret. After uncovering the nature of the shrouded histories, adventures head inside the secret refuge within Don Cavalier's office. Within this refuge, adventurers set out to solve the final mystery of Don Cavalier... the once President of Megalo Company, and the founder Trickster.
