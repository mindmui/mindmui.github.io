Quick Bar and Option

Minor quick bar and options changes.


Death Punishment and Portal Level Lock

No consequence death for low levels, and a level 60 lock on Northwest Forest.


Alert System

Exclamation Points will pop-up and tell you when you have reached a level to do certain sets of quests, like episode or key quests.


Equipment Comparison

Equipments tell you your stat shift, before you equip them, i.e. you have equiped a +40 AP sword and mouse over a +60 AP sword, it shows you a green up arrow and 20. Also cleans up description page info.


Unlimited Normal Quests

Unlimited Normal quests, you can accept as many normal quests as you are willing to take.


Night Vision

Night vision expanded.


Speed

Speed increased.
