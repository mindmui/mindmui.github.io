Description

The Fox is the female counterpart of the Sense Type. The Fox uses Knife type weapons for melee damage and Detection Ability (DA) to strengthen its attacks. The Fox specializes in powerful one-to-one attack skills but also offers other useful skills that allow the Fox to attack with shurikens or bags of poison.


Appearance

A female character with smart, stylish looks and cool tranquil personality. She has more than one tail which sets her apart from other animals.


Reason for coming to Caballa Island

Veronica is an archaeologist with sharp intuition, logics, and also a very rational person. She was the youngest person ever to earn a PhD in archaeology and soon becomes famous for all her works and findings in her field. When Veronica finds out about a new mysterious island that popped up in the middle of the Pacific Ocean, she wants to explore the island but is beaten by Don Cavalier. Her entrance to the competition is just an excuse to explore and study the island.

Hair Dye Colors

Hair color of your Fox can be determined upon creation of her, but only the basic colors are available to choose from. If you want to change the color, you can purchase a Hair Dye item from MyShop. Once the item is used, your Fox will always have that color hair in that job, unless changed later. When advancing to the next jobs however (second and third), they will use the default color (Purple) unless another Hair Dye item is purchased and used.

Notes: Please note there are different hair dye items for each character and job when purchasing from MyShop.
