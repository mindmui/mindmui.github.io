La Vida - Hunt Siren

[edit]
NPC: La Vida Location: Event Garden - Ceremonia
Quest Cycles: Each 25 Time(s) Condition: Level 15+
Level Request Reward Base / TM
15~40 15x Siren (20) (Lv.20) in 15 min Love Advice 3,315 / 2,124
41~70 15x Siren (50) (Lv.50) in 15 min Love Advice 40,448 / 43,156
71~100 20x Siren (80) (Lv.80) in 15 min Love Advice 145,257 / 339,108
101~140 20x Siren (120) (Lv.120) in 25 min Love Advice 372,904 / 466,976
141+ 20x Siren (160) (Lv.160) in 25 min Love Advice 834,026 / 624,830
Found at:
Siren (20) is found at Beach Field 6 - Statue of Anubis and Beach Field 2 - Center.

Siren (50) is found at Relics Field 1 - Atlas and Relics Field 4 - Altar of Round.

Siren (80) is found at Swamp Field 1 - Black Hole and Swamp Field 4 - Dark Omen.

Siren (120) is found at Rose Field 2 - Maze of Black Rose and Rose Field 3 - Maze of Blue Rose.

Siren (160) is found at Snow Field 1 - Merry Christmas and Snow Field 4 - Happy New Year.
