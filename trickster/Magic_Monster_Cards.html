Magic Monster Cards are standard Magic elemental type Card Battle cards. They are dropped by monsters throughout the game, can be acquired by certain Quests, bought or traded between players or won through Card Battle. They can be used for Card Identification, while some of them are required to master specific skills or needed to complete Card Girl's Quests.


Below is a list of the categorized Magic Monster Cards by Rank.
