The Proposal

First you need to couple a character of the opposite gender and gather Love Points through PQs, MQs, Memos or just spending time with your spouse in a party. Once you've gathered enough Love Points you will get notified through an in-game notification system and your My View window will start flashing. This will indicate that you may start preparing for your wedding by doing the following Wedding Quests:

After completing Kyu's quest, you can choose:

  1. Wedding Rings:
    1. Diamond Ring (70,000G)
    2. Opal Ring (55,000G)
    3. Pearl Ring (55,000G)
    4. Ruby Ring (60,000G)
    5. Sapphire Ring (60,000G)
  2. Wedding Titles:
    1. Cares For
    2. Dear Love
    3. Destiny
    4. Envied By All
    5. Heavenly Love
    6. Love
    7. Madly in Love
    8. Happy Smiling Couple
    9. (In game name)'s
  3. Wedding Attires: (if you pick a MyShop Attire, like Basic Wedding Clothes 2 or Luxurious Attire 12, you can preview the attire).


Notes: The choices above only needs to be done on one character, groom or bride. Also, if the couple bought both the MyShop Ring and MyShop Attire, then these has to be on either the groom or bride.


The Wedding Ring (Skill) Coupon 1 (2,000 Points) in MyShop gives a 10% LK boost skill. The Wedding Attires in MyShop (1,500~3,000 Points) are only usable during a wedding and will be consumed upon use.


The Reservations

Once you've chosen a ring and or an attire talk to Kyu at Event Garden - Ceremonia. He will teleport you to the Wedding Hall, there you can make reservations for your wedding.


Talk to Kyu in Wedding Hall and choose the first option to start. Click on "write" to prepare your wedding and reserve the desired hall. There are two halls to choose from, Megalo Hall or Caballa Hall. You can make it a private wedding by adding a password. Guests can add themselves to a public wedding by clicking on the reservation of the wedding they wish to attend. The guest limit for a wedding is 100 people.


On the left hand side of the reception hall you'll find Tuxedo T. He sells Heart Spring, Angel's Firework and Fairy's Firework (one time use skill scrolls) for 10,000 Galders each.


Notes: You can't access your inventory or open other windows once the ceremony starts, which is 5 minutes after you enter the Wedding Chapel. Do not open up the chat window after the ceremony is about to start or it will be stuck there.


The Wedding

Talk to Kyu in Wedding Hall to go directly to the Chapel. You will be prompted to write a message to your spouse at this time. Wedding ceremony will then start in 5 minutes.


The ceremony is conducted by High Priest of Poseidon. After the ceremony you will get an announcement saying you've been married. After your wedding ceremony ends you are able to stay for 20 minutes in the Wedding Chapel before you get teleported back to the Wedding Hall.


Talk to Kyu to receive your ring skill after the ceremony. From here on, you have the ability to find the location of your partner and use the Couple Teleport to teleport to your partner at any time (there are some exceptions, like Boss Rooms, Fiesta, Instanced Rooms and other special areas). The Couple tab in My View (Press V and click "Couple") will, aside from showing you how long you've been together as a couple, now will hold a record of how long you have been married as a couple.


Notes: You won't get a Ring (Accessory), the ring will be in a form of a Skill which will be in your Skill window (Press S and click "Special").


If the bride or groom exits the Wedding Chapel everyone will be kicked out.


Wedding Skills

Which Wedding Skill you get depends solely on the ring you've selected before your wedding, there are currently 6 rings available.


In-Game Wedding Skills:

  1. Balloon Heart (Opal Ring). This skill will make heart-shaped balloons appear above your spouse's head.
  2. Flying Hearts (Sapphire Ring). This skill will cause an explosion of winged hearts around your spouse.
  3. Gem Heart (Ruby Ring). This skill will make a heart-shaped red gem appear above your spouse's head.
  4. Heart Cloud (Pearl Ring). This skill will make a cloud filled with hearts rain down upon your spouse.
  5. Lips (Diamond Ring). This skill will make lips appear above your spouse's head.


MyShop Wedding Skills:

  1. So Into You (Wedding Ring (Skill) Coupon 1). This skill will make an emote with heart-shaped eyes appear above your spouse's head and will also give a 10% LK boost for 120 seconds. This skill is bought from MyShop for 2,000 points.


Notes: The In-Game Wedding Skills are the same, but differs slightly in color (Red for Females and Pink for Males when cast). They don't give any special stats or boosts either unlike the MyShop Wedding Skills.

Divorcing

When you're no longer satisfied with your marriage you have the option to divorce. Go talk to Kyu at Event Garden - Ceremonia and ask for a divorce. Your partner will have to confirm the divorce by signing the divorce papers to terminate the relationship. If your partner has quit the game and you wish to divorce, the system will automatically terminate the relationship for you after a period of one month. Once you divorce, there will be a penalty in place which will last for a whole week (7 days) before you are able to get into another relationship with a character.


Other things which are worth to note are that you can't freeze a married character. After divorcing, you still have to wait for a week (7 days) before you'd be able to freeze that character. This only applies to characters that are married.
