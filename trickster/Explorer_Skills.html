These are the second job skills available to the Fox class.

Note: These are the third job skills available to the Hybrid Lion. You will see the appropriate TM levels for the hybrids next to their original levels.

Note: Keep in mind, when using the formulas, to change the percents to decimals. To do so, move the decimal two places to the left. Ex. 65% = .65, 150% = 1.50

Types of Skills:

  • Timer Skill
    • The skill is applied to the self or others for a given period.
    • This skill type usually consists of skills that change character attributes.
  • On/Off Skill
    • One time use skill.
    • An attack skill or attack magic spell would fall in this category.
  • Passive Skill
    • Once learned, the Passive Skills are in effect continuously without the use of MP or special movement.
    • Learning a special technique or skills that raise stats like MP and weight are Passive Skills.


Precise Pitch
Icon Name TM Level Type Learn Mastery Mastery Item Prerequisite
Precise Pitch 41 (Cyber Hunter:91) Timer 2 2 Ironclad Turtle Card x2 Shuriken Master (Lv.10)
This skill enhances Shuriken Master for a short period of time. When using this skill, damage that is inflicted upon the enemy by throwing a weapon at them is amplified. As the user gains more experience, the amount of damage also increases.
Formula SM Attack, with PP = (DA × SM Mag.Ratio) + (Throw AP ÷ 10 × SM Mag.Ratio × PP Mag.Ratio)
Notes ex: SM (Lv.M) with PP (Lv.M) = (DA × 33) + (Throw AP ÷ 10 × 79.2)

Item Detector
Icon Name TM Level Type Learn Mastery Mastery Item Prerequisite
Item Detector 55 (Cyber Hunter:105) Timer 4 3 Quiem Card x2 Invincible Drilling
Sixth Sense (Lv.10)
This skill allows the user to sense items that are buried within the ground. By using insight and perceptibility, one can predict the existence of items in the area. As the user gains more experience, the probability of detecting items increases. The depth will depend upon how well the user's Detect Ability is.
Formula Detection = DA × (Multiplier ÷ 10)
Notes Fail often at low levels, becomes reliable once mastered.

Fan of Knives
Icon Name TM Level Type Learn Mastery Mastery Item Prerequisite
Fan of Knives 130 (Cyber Hunter:180) On/Off 4 4 Cobra Flower Card x4 Precise Pitch (Lv.M)
Physical Skill. Allows the player to attack multiple targets by throwing several thrown weapons. Player can throw up to a maximum of seven shuriken.
Formula FoK Attack = (DA + Throw AP ÷ 10) × AP%
FoK Attack with PP = (DA + ([ Throw AP ÷ 10 ] × 2.4)) × AP%
Notes AoE's range is erratic, similar to Entertainer's Siren Song or Light Magician's Light Wave. However, it fans out from the user rather than attacking in a cone shape behind the first target.
