Category:Use

From ggFTW Trickster Wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

Use Items are items that disappear after use and can be stacked like Etc. Items. Effects may include HP Recovery, MP Recovery or an increase in Galder. Other Use Items, like Ammo, can only be used at a certain time or when certain a requirement is met.


Some Use Items like Watermelon Sundae can only be obtained during a Event.


Here you find all Use Items distributed by alphabetical order. Please, see below.
