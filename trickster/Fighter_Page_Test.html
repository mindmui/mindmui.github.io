Description

The Buffalo is the male counterpart of the Power Type. The Buffalo uses Sword type weapons for melee damage and Attack Power (AP) to strengthen its attacks. The Buffalo has a large variety of multiple-target attack skills, along with some other single-target attacks. The Buffalo can also use Elemental Attributes to power up some of his skills.


Appearance

A fierce and powerful competitor working on your side, and a respectable and powerful opponent when heâ€™s not.


Reason for coming to Caballa Island

He is a professional fighter who would challenge and fight anybody. He unknowingly competes in an illegal fight and seriously injures his opponent in a win. But only finds out about it after he was cheated off his prize money by his manager. He comes to Caballa Island to make money to take care of his injured opponent and his family.


Skills

For more information see Power Skills, or click one of the below skills for in-depth information.

The Bunny and Buffalo share a set of Power Type skills in their first job. It consists of one-to-one attack skills and some stat buffs/debuffs.

Hair Dye Colors

Hair color of your Buffalo can be determined upon creation of his, but only the basic colors are available to choose from. If you want to change the color, you can purchase a Hair Dye item from MyShop. Once the item is used, your Buffalo will always have that color hair in that job, unless changed later. When advancing to the next jobs however (second and third), they will use the default color (red) unless another Hair Dye item is purchased and used.

Fashion

Default wears of your Buffalo (Fighter) are: Yellow Hoodie, Green Skirt, Orange Sneakers, and Red Gloves. If you want to change the wears, you can purchase and equipped a Fashion item or a Fashion Set from MyShop. When advancing to the next jobs however (second and third), they will use the job default wears, unequipped the first job wears. If you revert to your first job look (Schoolgirl) to Louis Bitton you can equipped the first job wears.
