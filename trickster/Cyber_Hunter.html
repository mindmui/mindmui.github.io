Introduction

With the assistance of the Explorer abilities the Lion becomes more balanced with the ability to choose its fighting style. Also drilling and compounding ability improves.

Skills

Cyber Hunter skills are similar to Explorer skills, however their TM level requirements are different. To figure out the difference add 50 TM levels to all base of the original TM level. For more information see Cyber Hunter Skills, or click one of the below skills for in-depth information.


Precise Pitch Item Detector Aura of Luck Chain of Knives Gas Mask
Landmine Sticky Icky Lightning Rod Pouch of Pain Fan of Knives


Hair Dye Colors

Upon job advancing, all new Cyber Hunters will start out with a default yellow hair color. If you want to change your hair color, you can purchase a Hair Dye item from MyShop. Once the item is used, your character will always have that color hair in that job, unless changed later. If you revert back to a previous job however, you will use whichever hair color that job was set at.


Basic Colors
Super Tender Color 2,700 Points


Notes: Please note there are different hair dye items for each character and job when purchasing from MyShop.
