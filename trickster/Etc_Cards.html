Etc. Cards are a mix of Power, Magic, Sense, Charm and Neutral elemental type of cards. They can be obtained from Card Girl's Quests, Events, bought or traded between players or won through Card Battle. Some of them can be used for Card Battle, exchanged for in-game items or used as a throwing item.


Below is a list of the categorized Etc. Cards by Rank.
