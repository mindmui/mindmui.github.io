Secret Cards are cards found through Card Identification and from Dimension Card Pack.


Each Secret Card unlocks an area in the Secret Space Map. There are 16 Secret Cards in total, once all 16 have been obtained, the map becomes complete (Secret Space Map). Using the Secret Space Map the character can access Janus's Secret Hideaway - Memory of Flames that allows to earn Summon Guardian skill (see Soul Guardian Quests Guide).
