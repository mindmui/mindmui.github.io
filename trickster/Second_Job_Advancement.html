Second Job Advancement

From ggFTW Trickster Wiki

Jump to: navigation, search
Bunny Buffalo Sheep Dragon Fox Lion Cat Raccoon


Requirements and Basic Info

Monster Kaboom
  • The minimum requirements for 2nd job advancement are base level 60 and TM level 50.
  • You can job change at later levels, with no penalty other than being unable to learn the new skills.
  • To job change, you must obtain 3x 100k Galder Check, which can be bought from Andrew in Relics Town - Azteca or in the Megalopolis Bank for 105,000 or a total of 315,000 Galders.
  • You must also hunt a Growth Badge from the monster Kaboom, who is located in Caballa Relics Dungeon 4.
  • Then, you can give these items to your respective Job Master. You will receive 1,000,000 base experience and 12,000,000 TM experience.
  • After advancing, your character's sprite art will change to their new job look, and your character is now able to wear capes.
  • At any time you can revert to your first job look by paying 50,000 galders to Louis Bitton, and 50,000 galders again to change back to second job look.

NPC Locations

Louis Bitton
  • The NPC's that provide the second Job Change can be found at Relics Town - Azteca, or Room of Job Master 1 and Room and Job Master 2, which are located in Garden of Skill Master. The portal to this area is located on the east-side of Megalopolis Square.
  • The Disguise NPC Louis Bitton can be found by the fountain in Megalopolis Square or next to the Job Masters in Relics Town - Azteca or in Swamp Town - Carbigal.

Job Master: Warrior Kei Advances from Fighter to Warrior
Quest Items: Growth Badge 3 x 100k Galder Check
Reward: Sign of Sword 1,000,000 EXP / 12,000,000 TM EXP
Description: Warriors specialize in attacking a large amount of enemies.
Although they are weaker in power than Boxers, Warriors are able to take on multiple enemies.

Magic Type

Sheep

Job Master: Bard La Fimmel Advances from Librarian to Bard
Quest Items: Growth Badge 3 x 100k Galder Check
Reward: Sign of Sound 1,000,000 EXP / 12,000,000 TM EXP
Description: Bards are able to use elemental magic based upon Fire, Water, Wind, Earth, and Thunder.
Fire, Water, and Thunder are elements that deal damage to a single enemy. Earth and Wind deal damage to multiple enemies.
Top of Page


Dragon

Job Master: Magician Louie Advances from Shaman to Magician
Quest Items: Growth Badge 3 x 100k Galder Check
Reward: Sign of Magical Tricks 1,000,000 EXP / 12,000,000 TM EXP
Description: Magicians use Dark or Light magic. Magicians who use Dark magic have stronger attack spells.
Those who use Light magic have stronger healing spells.

Quest Items: Growth Badge 3 x 100k Galder Check
Reward: Sign of Excavation 1,000,000 EXP / 12,000,000 TM EXP

Quest Items: Growth Badge 3 x 100k Galder Check
Reward: Sign of Popularity 1,000,000 EXP / 12,000,000 TM EXP
Description: Entertainers have skills that utilize their high physical strength and skills that use galders.
Entertainers have skills that allow her to transform into another creature.
