This is a guide for the new quests in Caballa Island, mainly Tower of Chaos's, after the Patch 2.84.2 (Dec. 08, 2011).


If you looking for how to obtain the Tower of Chaos's Titles (new "Title" tab on My View, "Honor" button), click here.


There is a break room every 6th floor, so on floors 1, 7, 13, 19, 25, 31, 37, 43, 49, 55, 61, and 67.


There is a boss on every 12th floor, so on floors 12, 24, 36, 48, 60, and 72.
