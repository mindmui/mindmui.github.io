Jump to: navigation, search
Official Event Page: Love Struck Item Girl!
Period: Feb. 08 ~ 22, 2012

Contents

  • 1 Item Boy Thomas - Item Boy Thomas' Request
  • 2 Card Girl - Love-Struck Item Girl
  • 3 Little Chagan - Crush? Just Leave it to Me!
  • 4 Item Girl - Mission for Item Girl to Confess
  • 5 Officer Tera - Case of the Attacked Couple
  • 6 Event Monsters
Total Base Experience:
  • 30~100: 598,603 + 23,005 (daily)
  • 101~200: 598,603 + 476,427 (daily)
  • 201+: 598,603 + 2,496,510 (daily)
Total TM Experience:
  • 30~100: 628,325 + 19,102 (daily)
  • 101~200: 628,325 + 596,615 (daily)
  • 201+: 628,325 + 1,078,258 (daily)
Prizes:
  • Thomas' Valentine Set
    • Thomas' Valentine Hat
    • Thomas' Valentine Weapon
    • Thomas' Valentine Shield
  • 50x 500 Galder Coupon
Items needed:
  • 50x Pilfered Pink Potion
  • 10x Ordinary Chocolate
  • 10x Hidden Flavors
  • 10x Brave D
Note: This event has 3 dialogues between 3 couples:
  • Favian & Vinosh (only dialogue)
  • Little Chagan & Pastor Tau (after complete Little Chagan - Crush? Just Leave it to Me!)
  • Item Girl & Officer Albert (after complete Item Girl - Mission for Item Girl to Confess)
