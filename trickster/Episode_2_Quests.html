Episode 2
Mirage Island
Regardless of Rosaline's warnings, adventurers hand over the Sword of Vengeance of the Vice President of Megalo Company, Don Giuvanni. While trying to acquire the Sword of Vengeance for Don Giuvanni, adventurers find out about the terrifying power of the Sword of Vengeance and about the sudden appearance of Mirage Island. Adventurers head out to Mirage Island to hear the old tragic story of 2 love birds that happened before the creation of Caballa Island... is this all relative?
