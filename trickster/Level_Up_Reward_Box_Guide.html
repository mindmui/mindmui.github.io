Level Up Reward Box Guide

From ggFTW Trickster Wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

Periodically, when your character levels up (base, not TM) a message will tell you that you have received a gift from Megalo Company. You can access this gift from the MyShop window.

Level Up Gift Box Rewards

Level 15

  • Teleport Guide
  • Lv. 15 Gift Box (1x Lotus Leaf Hat)

Level 20

  • Lv. 20 Gift Box (1x Megalo Pouch)

Level 40

  • Poppuri's Letter
  • Lv. 40 Gift Box (1x Flying Horse Department) 3 Days

Level 50

  • 2nd Job Guide (for your character type)
  • Lv. 50 Gift Box (1x 100k Galder Check)
  • Lv. Up Gift Box (Contains Random Myshop "Use" Items)

Level 80

  • Company Guide
  • Lv. 80 Gift Box A (30x First-Aid Pink Potion)
  • Lv. 80 Gift Box B (30x First-Aid Blue Potion)

Level 100

  • Nefertiti's Letter
  • 2x Lv. 100 Gift Box (1x Booster Bracer Ex per box, total of 2)
  • Lv. Up Gift Box (Contains Random Myshop "Use" Items)

Level 120

  • 3rd Job Guide (for your character type)
  • Lv. 120 Gift Box (2x Resurrect Scroll)

Level 140

  • Lv. 140 Gift Box (1x Snappy Sprint or 1x Gallant Sprint or 1x Flying Horse Department) 7 Days
  • Lv. Up Gift Box (Contains Random Myshop "Use" Items)

Level 160

  • Lv. 160 Gift Box (5x Gift Factory Pass)

Level 180

  • Lv. 180 Gift Box A (5x Tango Cabin Pass)
  • 4x Lv. 180 Gift Box B (1x Booster Bracer Ex per box, total of 4)
  • Lv. Up Gift Box (Contains Random Myshop "Use" Items)

Level 200

  • Lv. 200 Gift Box (5x Spicy Island Pass)
  • Lv. Up Gift Box (Contains Random Myshop "Use" Items)


Feel free correct any names, or add new rewards.
