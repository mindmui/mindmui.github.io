Requirements

Level Required

  • Base Level 130. TM Level 120.


Items Required

  • 1x Ticket of the Valiant (dropped by Clurican and Tenter Lion, or drilled in Swamp Dungeon)
  • 3x 1 Mil Galder Check (bought from Andrew at Megalopolis Bank)
  • 1x Harkon (drilled at Techichi Volcano Fields, Tapasco Volcano Fields, Chaos Tower Floors (Battlefields), Gate of Abyss, and Abyss Fields or from complete Blacksmith Marx - Find Ingredients for Precious Jewel and Blacksmith Marx - Silent Lava Mine Collection)
  • 1x Sacred Water (drilled in Black Swamp Fields)
  • 3x Brass (dropped from Brass Knight)
  • 3x Magnetite (dropped from Iron Knight)
  • 3x Magic Powder (dropped from Bone Magician)
  • 3x Glass Piece (dropped from Bone Warrior)
  • 1x Guardian Stone for your class (see quest 3 below)
  • Note: All other items required are obtained from other quests in the advancement. Keep in mind if you're going for Dark Lord ONLY you need everything above + 1x Adamantite, 2x Alexandrite, but no Harkon. If you were Dark Dragon as a 2nd job and are going Priest or Wizard you need a Harkon.

Rewards

  • 3x Flower of Revival
  • 1x Light Compass
  • 1x Accessory for your class (more information here).
  • 22,848,875 Base Exp and 48,848,875 TM Exp.

Quest 2 - Guardian's Messenger's 3rd Job Quest

NPC: Guardian's Messenger Location: Room of Guardian
Base Experience: None TM Experience: 16,000,000
Request: Harkon or Adamantite + Alexandrite (only for Dark Lord), Needleless Compass, Sealed Guardian
Quest Cycles: 1 Time Condition: Level 130+, TM Level 120+, have completed Door of Tribulation - Path of Tribulation
Cycle 1: Light Compass, Guardian (Accessory for your class, more information here)
Notes:
  • Harkon is obtained by complete Blacksmith Marx - Find Ingredients for Precious Jewel and Blacksmith Marx - Silent Lava Mine Collection, and is drilled in Techichi Volcano Fields, Tapasco Volcano Fields, Chaos Tower Floors (Battlefields), Gate of Abyss, and Abyss Fields
  • Adamantite is drilled at 1st Mining Lot of Precious Jewel and Tapasco Mine 1 - Silent Lava
  • Alexandrite is drilled at 2nd Mining Lot of Precious Jewel and Tapasco Mine 2 - Silent Lava
  • Needleless Compass is obtained by complete Quest 6 - Find 16 Guardian Stones for the Needleless Compass
  • Sealed Guardian is obtained by complete Quest 3 - Find 3rd Job Guardian Stone
  • After completion of this quest you become third job. At any time you can revert to your second job look by paying 50,000 Galders to Louis Bitton, and 50,000 Galders again to change back to third job look.
