Fairy Feria - Feria's Worry

[edit]
NPC: Fairy Feria Location: Snow Hill Gift Factory
Quest Cycles: Each 1 Time(s) Condition: Have completed Compounder Paul - Paul's Special Gift
Level Request Reward Base / TM
5~30 30x Sand Demon (Lv.20) in 20 min Soul Red Ribbon, Pink Ice Ring 45,000 / 53,000
31~60 30x Cucool (Lv.50) in 20 min Soul Red Ribbon, Pink Ice Ring 230,000 / 600,000
61~90 20x Raver Skeleton (Lv.90) in 15 min Soul Red Ribbon, Pink Ice Ring 1,500,000 / 1,500,000
91~120 2x Jelly Fish (Lv.130) in 20 min Soul Red Ribbon, Pink Ice Ring 3,300,000 / 2,300,000
121+ 2x Persona (Lv.180) in 20 min Soul Red Ribbon, Pink Ice Ring 7,300,000 / 4,300,000
Found at:
Sand Demon is found at Fiesta Zone 1 - Sand Alert.

Cucool is found at Fiesta Zone 2 - Cucool Alert.

Raver Skeleton is found at Fiesta Zone 3 - Skull Alert.

Jelly Fish is found at Fiesta Zone 4 - Jelly Alert.

Persona is found at Fiesta Zone 5 - Mask Alert.

Notes: Fiesta Zone 1 - Sand Alert can be reached at Beach Field 4 - Parasol River, Fiesta Zone 2 - Cucool Alert can be reached at Relics Field 2 - Hand of Giant, Fiesta Zone 3 - Skull Alert can be reached at Wharf Field 4 - Chrono Wharf, Fiesta Zone 4 - Jelly Alert can be reached at Seabed Field 2 - East, and Fiesta Zone 5 - Mask Alert can be reached at Rose Field 1 - Stairway to Sorrow. To make enter in these maps give 1x Fiesta Ticket to Robot GR-T21 (independent of Fiesta level restrictment).


If you want to know more about Ice Boy story talk to Al-Hauri at Gate of Desert Beach. He says to talk to Chief Koha (new name of Elder Qua) at Ghost Blue Seabed Town - Aquarius. Chief Koha then told more about Ice Boy story and says to talk to Captain Stan (at Aquarius) that offers free teleport to Cherry Blossom 2 (that map can be reached by Event Garden - Ceremonia too).
Top of Page

Notes

  1. During this event, in Snow Field 1 - Merry Christmas, Snowkid (Lv.198) and Snowman (Lv.200) are not active monsters (don't attack you, only attack if you attack them), and don't have trace of any Punisher Icicler (Lv.233).
  2. Robot GR-T21 stays in all maps that send you to Fiesta Zone Maps, independent of Fiesta level restrictment (obeying what monster Fairy Feria says to hunt).
  3. The depth of Snow Field 1 - Merry Christmas is 20~200m.
  4. Captain Stan at Ghost Blue Seabed Town - Aquarius offers free teleport to Cherry Blossom 2.
