NPC
Non-Player Characters are interactive characters in Trickster Online and various other game applications, notably RPG's, that provide either a backstory for the game or a task for the player to perform. Each town and most of its surrounding fields have NPC's waiting for the player to arrive to help them with some task or another, usually retrieving items.

NPC's have been organized by their location(s).
