Description: This is a reoccurring event for groups of tricksters where each trickster talk to Eclipse (using Mind's Eye), at a specific time to enter into a special room (Shadow Sanctuary) where you protect Harkon Statues from shadow monster attacks.

For 20 minutes the tricksters must protect 5 statues from waves of monster attacks. If the tricksters succeed, they are awarded, if they fail, they get nothing. The hint to succeed is coordination.

Prizes:
  • 1st Character with the most amount of monster kills:
    • Harkon Protector Fortune 3h boost (AP: +528, AC: +33, DX: -11, MA: +33, MD: +528, DA: +33, LK: +33, DP: +528, HV: +33)
  • Remained survivors:
    • Fortune 3h boost (AP: +168, AC: +7, MA: +7, MD: +168, DA: +7, LK: +7, DP: +168, HV: +7)
  • Monster drops: Shadow equipments, boss shards, HP & MP potions, galders
Requirement: Characters that has fully attained enlightenment. The first 30 character to talk to Eclipse for that area are accepted into that area's game (Shadow Sanctuary).


Enter in Shadow World

8 times of day Eclipse offers teleport to Shadow Sanctuaries. To see her you must use Mind's Eye. The time and location of where Harkon Statues are weakening are announced in-game at 10 minutes and 2 minutes before the Shadow World open.



The Shadow Sanctuary opens at the same times every day. However once a Harkon Statue is destroyed the Sanctuary is closed.

Eclipse will only appear when the path to the Shadow World opens. When this happen, she offers teleport to the region's Shadow Sanctuary.

Into Shadow Sanctuary

Once the character enter, you see to the right a mini map that shows the locations of the Harkon Statues and the time remaining.
