Poppuri Event

Together, everyone wins! Drill to find the special Poppuri's Treasure Boxes hidden underground throughout Caballa Island! Scattered all over the island, hidden in secret and sometimes obvious spots, special Poppuri's Treasure Boxes can be found! There’s no telling where you might find them, so it’s best to drill everywhere!


This is a group event, something for everyone to do together! There are several stages to the event, seven in total. With the completion of each stage, everyone will receive yet another prize! Here’s how it works, for each stage there is a particular number of boxes that must be found. Once all the boxes have been found to satisfy that stage, everyone at level 40 or above, and having at least 50 boxes will receive a special stage specific reward! The more everyone drills, the more everyone wins!
