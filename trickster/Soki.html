Level Restriction Spawn
Level 150+ Soki spawns in 45 min after the previous appearance.
Walkthrough
Give 2x Stone Ball (consumed) to Tango at Black Ash Dungeon 4.
To proceed to the boss, you have to complete the following trials:
  1. Trial 1 - Techichi Path to Soki 1 - Maze of Death
    1. Dig up 1x Blue Bone 1 (for map that has Blue Bones) or 1x Red Bone 1 (for map that has Red Bones). For Ponchichi locations on both maps please see this image map.
    2. Talk to 1st Ponchichi and receive 1x Blue Bone 2 or 1x Red Bone 2. Follow the image map to locate the other Ponchichis.
    3. Talk to 2nd Ponchichi (it takes Blue Bone 2/Red Bone 2) and receive 1x Blue Bone 3/Red Bone 3
    4. Talk to 3rd Ponchichi (it takes Blue Bone 3/Red Bone 3) and receive 1x Blue Bone 4/Red Bone 4
    5. Talk to 4th Ponchichi (it takes Blue Bone 4/Red Bone 4) and receive 1x Blue Bone 5/Red Bone 5
    6. Talk to 5th Ponchichi (it takes Blue Bone 5/Red Bone 5) and receive 1x Blue Bone 6/Red Bone 6
    7. Talk to 6th Ponchichi (it takes Blue Bone 6/Red Bone 6 and teleport to 7th Ponchichi area)
    8. Talk to 7th Ponchichi and answer 2 questions (see these guides: Blue Bone Map Questions / Red Bone Map Questions). After answer correctly both questions, it teleport to an area that has a portal to next trial map.
  2. Trial 2 - Techichi Path to Soki 2 - Origin of Death
    1. Talk to Compounder Nadia and complete Nadia's Request (hunt 3x Ashen Doom Swine (Lv.360).
    2. In farm area, dig up 1x Worn Magical Rune (you can drill more for the next compound part).
    3. Talk to Compounder Nadia and complete Matching Rune Stone 1
    4. Talk to Compounder Nadia and start Matching Rune Stone 2. Take some Incense Remnants from Ashen Doom Swine and drill some Worn Magical Rune.
    5. Talk to Compounder Nadia and compound 1x Worn Magical Rune + 1x Incense Remnants to obtain 1x Rune Stone.
    6. Talk to Marked Stone to complete Matching Rune Stone 2. It teleport to next trial map.
  3. Trial 3 - Techichi Path to Soki 3 - Death's Time
    1. Talk to Orpeo. She gives Bottle of Wishes and start the Monster Quest: A Women's Wish. After complete it, she teleport to next area.
    2. Talk to Orpeo and start the Monster Quest: The Killi's Wish. After complete it, she teleport to next area.
    3. Talk to Orpeo and start the Monster Quest: The Soldier's Wish. After complete it, she takes Bottle of Wishes and teleport to next trial map.
  4. Trial 4 - Techichi Path to Soki 4 - Test of Death
    1. Dig up 1x Cat Doll Part 1, 1x Cat Doll Part 2, 1x Cat Doll Part 3
    2. Talk to Brother Tango and complete Doll Parts for Brother Tango.
    3. Talk to Brother Tango again to start Soul for Brother Tango. It teleport to Doom Lion jail.
    4. Take 1x Techichi Village's Holy Sword from Doom Lion (Lv.410) (be careful, the lion has Banish).
    5. Give Techichi Village's Holy Sword to Brother Tango and it teleports to Black Ash Dungeon Boss Room
Notes
Soki now absorbs Physical damage (but you can use Elemental Melee to get around this) as well as Gun / Shuriken Damage (but you can use Shield Breaker to be able to damage him), and is weak to both elemental magic (all types) and non-elemental magic.
