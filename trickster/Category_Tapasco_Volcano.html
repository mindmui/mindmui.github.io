Tapasco Volcano is a town where molten lava bubbles and flows in the cracks of land. In this barren land nothing grows but pepper trees which can be found strewn about. Spicy ingredients and flavors can be found where gas and vapor erupt. That is why the name Tapasco was given.


Lv250 ~ Lv290. This region is for high level adventurers.
