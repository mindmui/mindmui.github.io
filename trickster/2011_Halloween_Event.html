2011 Halloween Event

From ggFTW Trickster Wiki

Jump to: navigation, search
Official Event Page: The Legend of the Pumpkin Ghost Village!
Period: Oct. 18, 2011 ~ Nov. 09, 2011

Contents

  • 1 Magician Sephira - Pumpking Ghost Rumors
  • 2 Majordomo Felix - Guard the Jack-O-Lantern! (Thursday)
  • 3 Butler of Vamp Castle - Witch Who Comes Out During the Day (Friday)
  • 4 Star Gazer Stella - Eerie Vibes (Saturday)
  • 5 Genius Cochma - What Pumpkin Ghost Like to Do (Sunday)
  • 6 Fortune Teller - Pumpkin Ghost's Clue (Monday)
  • 7 Tango - Find Me the Key (Tuesday)
  • 8 Vamp Lydia - Cleaning is Tiring (Wednesday)
  • 9 Magician Sephira - Exchange
Total Base Experience:
  • 50~100: 957,931
  • 101~150: 1,381,203
  • 150+: 2,021,267
Total TM Experience:
  • 50~100: 2,534,454
  • 101~150: 2,941,438
  • 150+: 3,178,428
Prizes:
  • Spider Set
    • Spider Ribbon
    • Spider Umbrella
    • Spider Shield
  • Frankenstein Hat
  • 50x 500 Galder Coupon
Items needed:
  • 40x Shiny Pumpkin Jewel (Friday)
  • 20x Ghost Memo (Saturday)
  • 20x Skull Necklace (Saturday)
  • 20x Sweet Apple Candy (Sunday)
  • 20x Sweet Pumpkin Candy (Sunday)
  • 40x Skull Keychain (Monday)
  • 2x Blooming Key (Tuesday)
  • 2x Paradise Key (Tuesday)
  • 2x Megalo Key (Tuesday)
  • 40x Halloween Trash (Wednesday)
Notes: Ghost Memo, Halloween Trash, Skull Keychain, Skull Necklace, Sweet Apple Candy, and Sweet Pumpkin Candy can be drilled everywhere during this event.
You can drill all these items and Halloween Gift Box in Closed Amusement Park - Megalo Land.
You can obtain Frankenstein Hat from Day Loving Witches and Night Loving Witches (extremely rare drop).
Halloween Gift Box's Contents:
  • Biscuit Explosion
  • Halloween Cookie
  • Halloween Candy
  • Halloween Chocolate
  • Halloween Caramel
