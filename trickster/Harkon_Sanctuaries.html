Harkon Sanctuaries

From ggFTW Trickster Wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

UNDER CONSTRUCTION


Official Page: Save the Sanctuaries!
Description: This is a reoccurring event for groups of low level tricksters where each trickster talk to Eclipse (don't need Mind's Eye), at a specific time to enter into a special room (Harkon Sanctuary) where you protect Harkon Statues from monster attacks.

For 20 minutes the tricksters must protect 5 statues from waves of monster attacks. If the tricksters succeed, they are awarded, if they fail, they get nothing. The hint to succeed is coordination.

  • Monster drops: Corrupt Souls, Essences of Evil, Chronos' Ancient Seals, galders
Requirement: The first 30 character to talk to Eclipse for that area are accepted into that area's game (Harkon Sanctuary).


Enter in Harkon Sanctuary

6 times of day Eclipse offer teleport to Harkon Sanctuaries, 2 times by range level (1st Harkon Sanctuary to Level 30~79; 2nd Harkon Sanctuary to Level 80~129; 3rd Harkon Sanctuary to Level 130~179). The weakening time of Harkon Statues are announced in-game at 10 minutes and 2 minutes before the Harkon Sanctuary open in Event Garden - Ceremonia. Happisto Stallone is in Ceremonia too and offers 3 quests.


Image:Harkon Sanctuary Broadcast.png


The Harkon Sanctuary opens at the same times every day. However once a Harkon Statue is destroyed the Sanctuary is closed.

Eclipse will only appear when the path to the Harkon Sanctuary opens. When this happen, she offers teleport to the respective Harkon Sanctuary.


Into Harkon Sanctuary

Once the character enter, you see to the right a mini map that shows the locations of the Harkon Statues and the time remaining.
