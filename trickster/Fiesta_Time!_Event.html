Fiesta
Reference: Fiesta Renewal!


What is a Fiesta Zone?

Fiesta Zones are maps that are filled with active and non-active monsters. EXP and TM bonuses are applied when you enter the Fiesta Zone. There are 10 Fiesta Zones divided in brackets over 400 levels. Each of them has a Waiting Room with an Item Girl selling the same items and a portal to the Ball Room map. This map has non-active monsters (Merrymakers) and a portal to Grand Ball Room that, in its turn, is filled with active monsters (Revellers). The portal to the Grand Ball Room will only appear after you've spend 5 minutes of the Fiesta time.


How to enter in a Fiesta Zone?

The Fiesta Zone will open every 2 hours throughout a day (the time is fixed), you can only enter twice a day. Once it's time, you'll get an in-game announcement saying: "The Fiesta Ticket is needed to enter the Waiting Room.", this is the time when you'll be able to click your Fiesta Ticket (consumed). This ticket will teleport you to your level bracket' Waiting Room. You can prepare yourself here for 5 minutes till the next announcement appears, which will state: "The Fiesta has started!", that's when the Ball Room portal will be unlocked so you can enter the map. You have exactly 20 minutes to kill as many monsters you can, after 5 minutes have passed the portal to the Grand Ball Room will appear on the map, so make sure you profit of the bonus EXP and TM! When the time is up you'll be teleported back to the map you were at before using your Fiesta Ticket.
