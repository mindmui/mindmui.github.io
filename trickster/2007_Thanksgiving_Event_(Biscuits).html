  • 90,131 (for Level 15~40)
  • 1,240,406 (for Level 41~70)
  • 2,863,081 (for Level 71~100)
  • 6,709,506 (for Level 101~140)
  • 15,073,181 (for Level 141+)
Total TM Experience:
  • 57,753 (for Level 15~40)
  • 3,257,078 (for Level 41~70)
  • 6,371,503 (for Level 71~100)
  • 8,494,178 (for Level 101~140)
  • 11,485,403 (for Level 141+)
Prizes:
  • Melon Set
    • Melon Hat
    • Melon Stick
    • Melon Covered Shield
    • Melon Biscuit
  • Strawberry Set
    • Strawberry Hat
    • Strawberry Stick
    • Berry Covered Shield
    • Strawberry Biscuit
  • ChocoAlmond Set
    • Almond Hat
    • ChocoAlmond Stick
    • ChocoAlmond Shield
    • ChocoAlmond Biscuit
  • Mighty Turkey
  • variable amounts of Turkey A
  • variable amounts of Turkey B
Items needed:
  • 1x 1st Dark Turkey Rune
  • 1x 2nd Dark Turkey Rune
  • 1x 3rd Dark Turkey Rune
  • 1x 4th Dark Turkey Rune
  • 1x Turkey A
  • 1x Turkey B
Notes: Great Turkey Coffer is drilled at everywhere. Its contents are:
  • 1st Dark Turkey Rune
  • 2nd Dark Turkey Rune
  • 3rd Dark Turkey Rune
  • 4th Dark Turkey Rune
  • 10x Pink Potion B
  • 10x Blue Potion B

Hunter Yuri - Hunt The Voodoo Monster

[edit]
NPC: Hunter Yuri Location: Event Garden - Ceremonia
Quest Cycles: Each 25 Time(s) Condition: Level 15+, have complete Magician Sephira - Dinner For Sephira in quest log
Level Request Reward Base / TM
15~40 15x Voodoo Shell Trap (Lv.22) in 15 min 5x Turkey A or 5x Turkey B or Strawberry Gift Box 2,486 / 1,593
41~70 15x Voodoo Forest Mantis (Lv.44) in 15 min 5x Turkey A or 5x Turkey B or Strawberry Gift Box 48,497 / 129,566
71~100 15x Voodoo Forest Wasp (Lv.77) in 15 min 5x Turkey A or 5x Turkey B or Strawberry Gift Box 113,404 / 254,143
101~140 15x Voodoo Stone Soldier (Lv.155) in 15 min 5x Turkey A or 5x Turkey B or Strawberry Gift Box 267,261 / 339,050
141+ 15x Voodoo Mimic (Lv.288) in 15 min 5x Turkey A or 5x Turkey B or Strawberry Gift Box 601,808 / 458,699
Found at:
Voodoo Shell Trap is found at Beach Field 2 - Center and Beach Field 6 - Statue of Anubis.

Voodoo Forest Mantis is found at Relics Field 1 - Atlas and Relics Field 4 - Altar of Round.

Voodoo Forest Wasp is found at Swamp Field 1 - Black Hole and Swamp Field 4 - Dark Omen.

Voodoo Stone Soldier is found at Rose Field 2 - Maze of Black Rose and Rose Field 3 - Maze of Blue Rose.

Voodoo Mimic is found at Snow Field 1 - Merry Christmas and Techichi Field 2.
Notes: The Voodoo Monsters dropped Melon Gift Box. Don't complete Sephira's quest right away so you can keep doing the monster quest. If you complete her quest you WON'T be able to do anymore of Yuri's quests.
