Official Content Guide: Surprise Spot Guide
Total Base Experience: 6,139
Total TM Experience: 5,900
Prizes:
  • 1x Treasure Map Use Guild
Items needed: None
NOTES: The info above is for unlocked Surprise Spots. The Surprise Spot Quests of Weird and Old Maps are Daily Quests and Worn Maps can do infinite times. These quests are available in all regions (except Coral Beach, Phantom School, and Abyss). Explorer Reina offer Normal Quests for Worn and Old Maps and Driller King Marky offer Normal Quests for Weird Maps. The Old Map quests only can be do after finished doing the Worn Map quest 50 times in one location. For example, you can only do the Old Map quest in Snow Hill daily after you finished the Worn Map Quest 50 times in Snow Hill. Which means you can't do the Old Map quest anywhere else unless you have finished the Worn Map Quest in that location.
