Honor Titles

You can obtain these titles by completing the Tower of Chaos's Quests. You got ranking when you complete a certain amount of attainments.


# Title Explanation
1 Oddballs Friend Title given to explorers who understand Wandering Photographer Ren's hobby. Granted for completing Wandering Photographer Ren - Flyer Picture Request (2).
2 Nice Adventurer Title given to the person who gets Professor Komby to tell them the secret. Granted for completing Professor Komby - Suspicious Intruder Info.
3 Mercenary Recruiter Title given to an adventurer who negotiates a mercenary contract. Granted for completing Developer Project Manager L - Poirot Entrance Promise.
4 Smarty Assistant Title given to the player who brings an item the Inventor PM was griping about. Granted for completing Don Giuvanni - Deliver the Action Log Info.
5 Committee volunteer Title given to the explorer who receives the Tower of Chaos Pass. Granted for completing the Developer Project Manager L - Investigating Committee Work.
6 G's Friend Title given to the people that helped G, who was left alone at the Tower of Chaos. Granted for completing Part-Timer G - Collect Chaos Integer.2Byte.
7 2Bit Inventor Title given to explorers who realized the inventor's capabilities after collecting enough chaos integers. It's only 2Bit but it's a start. Granted for completing Part-Timer G - Collect Chaos Integer.2Byte, 10 times.
8 Aspiring Mercenary Title given to adventurers who receive a proficiency test as soon as they enter the Tower of Chaos. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Stage 2 Proficiency Test.
9 Mercenary Trainee Title given to adventurers who receive harsh training as soon as they enter the Tower of Chaos. Granted for hunting more than 105 monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Harsh Training (2).
10 Committee Slacker Title given to the explorers who waste time at the lower levels of Tower of Chaos instead of climbing it. Granted for completing Part-Timer G - Collect Chaos Integer.2Byte, 20 times.
11 4Byte Inventor Title given to adventurers who realize the inventor's capabilities after chaos integer collection. It's still early, so it's only 4Byte. Granted for completing Part-Timer G - Collect Chaos Integer.4Byte, 15 times.
12 Smack Worthy Title given to explorers who fight against monsters that don't feel pain even though they're smacked. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Harsh Training (5).
13 Vaccine Inventor Title given to adventurers who invent something to fend off the latest 64bit virus. Granted for completing Don Giuvanni - Take the Mysterious Video Disk.
14 Noob Assistant Title given to explorers who help Poirot with the survey and report the initial results. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Deliver the Completed 1st Survey Notebook.
15 Entomologist Title given to explorers who are knowledgeable about insects after hunting so many of them. Granted for hunting more than 200 monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Harsh Training (8).
16 8KB Inventor Title given to explorers who realized the inventor's capabilities after collecting enough chaos integers. 8KB, not much to brag about. Granted for completing Part-Timer G - Collect Chaos Integer.8Byte, 20 times.
17 10th floor Explorer Title given to adventurer who successfully explored up to the Tower of Chaos 10th Floor. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Go Survey the 11th Floor.
18 Requiem Discoverer Title given to explorers who discover the existence of Requiem Monsters. Granted for completing Poirot - 12th Floor Dimensional Gap Investigation.
19 Sweet Talker Title given to the explorer who receives the gift of sweet talking while hunting Corrupt Thiefmon. Granted for hunting more than 150 monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Stage 15 Proficiency Test.
20 16MB Inventor Title given to explorers who realized the inventor's capabilities after collecting enough chaos integers. It's getting better as it's 16MB. Granted for completing Part-Timer G - Collect Chaos Integer.16Byte, 25 times.
21 Bald Title given to the explorer who goes bald because of Corrupt Niwerth's trance. Granted for hunting more than 300 monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Harsh Training (17).
22 Bad Explorer Title given to explorer who can decipher the bad code. Granted for completing Part-Timer G - Collect Chaos Integer.18Byte.
23 20th floor Explorer Title given to adventurer who successfully explored up to the Tower of Chaos 20th Floor. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Go Survey the 21st Floor.
24 Requiem Researcher Title given to explorers who research Requiem Monsters. Granted for completing Poirot - 24th Floor Dimensional Gap Investigation.
25 Kochi's Best Client Title given to the explorer who's the biggest client of the Tower of Chaos teleporting services. Granted for completing Kochi - Register for Tower of Chaos 25th Floor.
26 Part-timer Title given to the explorer who has proven to be better than the part-timer employee. Granted for completing Intern G - Collect Chaos Integer.26Byte, 10 times.
27 Carefree Musician Title given to explorers who are focused on their music after hunting many Corrupt Toy Soldiers. Granted for hunting more than 120 monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Stage 29 Proficiency Test.
28 30th floor Explorer Title given to adventurers who successfully explored up to the Tower of Chaos 30th Floor. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Deliver the Completed 5th Survey Notebook.
29 32GB Inventor Title given to explorers who realized the inventor's capabilities after collecting enough chaos integers. It's an alright size of 32GB. Granted for completing Intern G - Collect Chaos Integer.32Byte, 30 times.
30 Master Potter Title given to an explorer who shattered hundreds of badly made jars. Granted for hunting more than 200 monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Stage 34 Proficiency Test.
31 Lousy Explorer Title given to explorer who can decipher the lousy code. Granted for completing Intern G - Collect Chaos Integer.36Byte.
32 Requiem Investigator Title given to explorers who research Requiem Monsters. Granted for completing Poirot - 36th Floor Dimensional Gap Investigation.
33 Novice Detective Title given to the adventurer who help Poirot with the survey and a follow-up results report. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Deliver the Completed 6th Survey Notebook.
34 40th floor Explorer Title given to adventurer who successfully explored up to the Tower of Chaos 40th Floor. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Go Survey the 41st Floor.
35 Cat Lover Title given to the adventurer who keeps hunting Chaos Sets with no regards for scratches. Granted for hunting more than 160 monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Stage 42 Proficiency Test.
36 Pachi's Best Client Title given to the explorer who's the biggest client of the Tower of Chaos teleporting services. Granted for completing Pachi - Register for Tower of Chaos 43rd Floor.
37 Book Worm Title given to the explorer who's thought to like books due to hunting so many Chaos Ghost Books. Granted for hunting more than 180 monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Stage 46 Proficiency Test.
38 Decent Explorer Title given to the explorer who can decipher the decent code. Granted for completing Intern G - Collect Chaos Integer.48Byte.
39 Requiem Hunter Title given to the explorer who successfully defeated the substance of Requiem. Granted for completing Poirot - 48th Floor Dimensional Gap Investigation.
40 Intern Title given to the adventurer who's shown better capabilities than the intern. Granted for completing Rookie G - Collect Chaos Integer.50Byte, 10 times.
41 Fast Explorer Title given to the adventurer who quickly hunts monsters with guard abilities. Granted for hunting more than 100 monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Stage 50 Proficiency Test.
42 50th floor Explorer Title given to adventurer who successfully explored up to the Tower of Chaos 50th Floor. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Go Survey the 51st Floor.
43 Big Merchant Title given to an adventurer who receives a big merchant energy while hunting Chaos Part-timer Mon. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Harsh Training (51), 20 times.
44 Fiery Barefoot Title given to the adventurer who wandered around Chaos Aposis' Turf. Granted for hunting more than 150 monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Harsh Training (54).
45 Prison Breaker Title given to the adventurer who escaped the ice prison many times. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Stage 56 Proficiency Test, 22 times.
46 Marksman Title given to the adventurer who becomes a marksman while hunting the Chaos Coal Soldiers. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Stage 59 Proficiency Test, 30 times.
47 Great Explorer Title given to explorer who can decipher the great code. Granted for completing Rookie G - Collect Chaos Integer.60Byte.
48 60th Floor Explorer Title given to adventurer who successfully explored up to the Tower of Chaos 60th Floor. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Deliver the Completed 11th Survey Notebook.
49 Requiem Chaser Title given to adventurer who successfully hunted the core of Requiem. Granted for completing Poirot - 60th Floor Dimensional Gap Investigation.
50 Outraged Explorer Title given to the adventurer who quickly hunts monsters with guard abilities. Granted for hunting more than 200 monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Harsh Training (63).
51 64TB Inventor Title given to explorers who realized the inventor's capabilities after collecting enough chaos integers. It's a considerable amount at 64TB. Granted for completing Rookie G - Collect Chaos Integer.64Byte, 35 times.
52 Infinite Enchanter Title given to adventurer who continues fighting regardless of Chaos Crow's curse. Granted for hunting more than 180 monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Harsh Training (65) Quest.
53 Hard Worker Title given to adventurer who obtained the skills from Chaos Raver Skeleton. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Harsh Training (66) Quest, 24x.
54 Skunk Hunter Title given to the adventurer who's used to getting spayed by skunks. Granted for hunting more than 150 monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Harsh Training (69).
55 Death Avoider Title given to the adventurer who's immune to Chaos Tanya's curse. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Harsh Training (70) Quest 20x.
56 70th Floor Explorer Title given to adventurers who successfully explored up to the Tower of Chaos 70th Floor. Granted for completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Go Survey the 71st Floor.
57 Rookie Title given to the adventurer who's shown better capabilities than a rookie. Granted for completing Rookie G - Collect Chaos Integer.72Byte, 10 times.
58 Holy Explorer Title given to explorer who can decipher the great code. Granted for completing Rookie G - Collect Chaos Integer.72Byte.
59 Tower Conqueror Title given to the explorer who has climbed to the top of Tower of Chaos. Granted for completing Poirot - Defeat Outraged Requiem.
60 Warrior's Successor Title given to the explorer who has finished Wandering Warrior Tan's training. Granted for hunting more than [400] monsters while completing Wandering Warrior Tan - Harsh Training (72) Quest.

Set Titles

You can obtain these titles by equip all items that belongs a set.

Boss Sets

Pharaoh

Pharaoh Set


Bonus: MP +245 WT +350 HP +245 DP +70 HV +4
Hat
Special
 Shield


 Acc


How to obtain:
The Pharaoh Set is completed by hunting Tutankhamen (Lv.71) for the Pharaoh Hat, Golden Sword and Golden Lion Shield and completing the subsequent questline for the Ankh Pendant.
Notes:
  • The Hat, Sword and Shield can be acquired by hunting Tutankhamen at level 35~90.
  • The Ankh Pendant's quests can be completed at level 35~90; characters lower than 35 and higher than 90 cannot acquire the Pendant.
  • All pieces, except the Pendant, can be refined. All pieces can be compounded. See individual pages for details.
  • The Hat, Sword and Shield are tradeable; the Pendant is not.
  • The Pendant can be stored and transferred through the Bank.

The Guardian Set is completed by hunting Tombeth (Lv.121) for the Guardian's Soul Helm, Guardian's Soul Staff and Guardian's Soul Shield and completing the subsequent questline for the Guardian's Soul Pendant.
Notes:
  • The Helm, Staff and Shield can be acquired by hunting Tombeth at level 1~150.

  • All pieces, except the Pendant, can be refined. All pieces can be compounded. See individual pages for details.
  • The Helm, Staff and Shield are tradeable; the Pendant is not.
  • The Pendant can be stored and transferred through the Bank.

How to obtain:
The Pirate Set is completed by hunting Pirate King Karan (Lv.250) for the Ebon's Admiral Hat and Pearl Pistol and completing the subsequent questline for the Blood-Red Eyepatch.
Notes:
  • The Hat and Gun can be acquired by hunting Pirate King Karan at level 1~280.
  • The Blood-Red Eyepatch's quests can be completed at level 1~280; characters higher than 280 cannot acquire the Eyepatch.

  • The Hat, Gun and Eyepatch (Face Accessory) are tradeable.
