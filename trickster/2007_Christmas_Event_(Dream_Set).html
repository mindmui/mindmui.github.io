Witch La Befana - La Befana's Cookie

[edit]
NPC: Witch La Befana Location: Event Garden - Ceremonia
Quest Cycles: Each 25 Time(s) Condition: Have Fairy Asrai - Toy Filled With Dreams in quest log
Level Request Reward Base / TM
1~40 15x Jolly Thief Mon 20 (Lv.20) in 15 min R Ticket, Christmas Box 5,852 / 4,222
41~70 15x Jolly Thief Mon 50 (Lv.50) in 20 min R Ticket, Christmas Box 40,776 / 115,351
71~100 15x Jolly Thief Mon 80 (Lv.80) in 20 min R Ticket, Christmas Box 118,167 / 176,69
101~140 15x Jolly Thief Mon 120 (Lv.120) in 25 min R Ticket, Christmas Box 267,998 / 255,762
141+ 15x Jolly Thief Mon 160 (Lv.160) in 25 min R Ticket, Christmas Box 694,269 / 357,952
Found at:
Jolly Thief Mon 20 is found at Beach Field 2 - Center and Beach Field 6 - Statue of Anubis.

Jolly Thief Mon 50 is found at Relics Field 1 - Atlas and Relics Field 4 - An Alter for Sacrifice.

Jolly Thief Mon 80 is found at Swamp Field 1 - Black Hole and Swamp Field 4 - Dark Omen.

Jolly Thief Mon 120 is found at Rose Field 2 - Maze of Black Rose and Rose Field 3 - Maze of Blue Rose.

Jolly Thief Mon 160 is found at Snow Field 1 - Merry Christmas and Snow Field 4 - Happy New Year.
Notes: Witch La Befana offers free teleport to Coral Town - Blooming Cora.
