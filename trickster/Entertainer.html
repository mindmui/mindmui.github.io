Introduction

Entertainers have skills that utilize their high physical strength and skills that use galders. Entertainers have skills that allow them to transform into another creature.


Skills

For more information see Entertainer Skills, or click one of the below skills for in-depth information.

Aura Shatter


Hair Colors

Upon job advancing, all new Entertainers will start out with a default yellow hair color. If you want to change the color, you can purchase a Hair Dye item from MyShop. Once the item is used, your character will always have that color hair in that job, unless changed later. When advancing to the third job however, they will again use the default color unless another Hair Dye item is purchased and used. If you revert back to first job, you will use whichever hair color that job was set at.

Notes: Please note there are different hair dye items for each character and job when purchasing from MyShop.


Advance to 3rd Job

Level Required:

  • Base Level 130. TM Level 120.

Items Required:

  • 1x Ticket of the Valiant (dropped by Clurican)
  • 3x 1 Mil Galder Check (bought from Andrew at Megalopolis Bank)
  • 1x Harkon (drilled at Techichi Volcano Fields, Tapasco Volcano Fields, Chaos Tower Floors (Battlefields), Gate of Abyss, and Abyss Fields or from complete Blacksmith Marx - Find Ingredients for Precious Jewel and Blacksmith Marx - Silent Lava Mine Collection)
  • 1x Sacred Water (drilled in Black Swamp Fields)
  • 3x Brass (dropped from Brass Knight)
  • 3x Magnetite (dropped from Iron Knight)
  • 3x Magic Powder (dropped from Bone Magician)
  • 3x Glass Piece (dropped from Bone Warrior)
  • 1x Rodonite (dropped from Beast Vincento) for Primadonna or
  • 1x Dusk Amethyst (dropped from Fish Guardian Tink) for Diva
  • Note: All other items required are obtained from other quests in the advancement.

Rewards:

  • 3x Flower of Revival
  • 1x Light Compass
  • 1x Rodonite Guardian (Primadonna) or Amethyst Guardian (Diva)
  • 22,848,875 Base Exp and 48,848,875 TM Exp.
