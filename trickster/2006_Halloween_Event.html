Official Event Page: Halloween
Period: Oct. 26 ~ Nov. 06, 2006

Contents

  • 1 Warrior Kei - Power-Type Monster Quest
  • 2 Magician Louie - Magic-Type Monster Quest
  • 3 Explorer Reina - Sense-Type Item Collection Quest
  • 4 Entertainer Elicia - Charm-Type Item Collection Quest
  • 5 Witch La Befana - Exchange
  • 6 Magician Sephira - Exchange
  • 7 Compounder Nadia - Compound
Total Base Experience:
  • 212,000 (for Level 5~20)
  • 400,000 (for Level 21~55)
  • 500,000 (for Level 56~85)
  • 600,000 (for Level 86+)
Total TM Experience:
  • 212,000 (for Level 5~20)
  • 400,000 (for Level 21~55)
  • 500,000 (for Level 56~85)
  • 600,000 (for Level 86+)
Prizes:
  • Witch's Trick Ring
  • Witch's Treat Ring
  • Trick Horn Hair Band
  • Treat Pumpkin On-Top
  • variable amounts of Gale Card
  • variable amounts of Alicia Card
  • variable amounts of Taniel Card
  • variable amounts of Louis Bitton Card
  • variable amounts of 500 Galder Coupon
  • variable amounts of Halloween Candy
  • variable amounts of Halloween Cookie
  • variable amounts of Halloween Chocolate
  • variable amounts of Halloween Caramel
Items needed:
  • 10x Warez Yummy Stick
  • 10x Little Cora Card
  • 10x Cannon Shell Card
  • 10x Cora Pyupyu Card
  • Wood Gear
  • Basic Cap
