Category:Quest

From ggFTW Trickster Wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

A Quest in Trickster is generally a task or series of tasks, which a player or group of players may complete in order to gain a reward. Rewards may include Exp, TM Exp, Items, Equipment, Drills, Pets, Cards, money, etc or any combination of the afore mentioned.

Currently, quests are organized by:

  • Quests by Level
  • Quests by Location
  • Quests by Type
