  • 2,729,239 (for Level 56~90)

  • 7,297,859 (for Level 121+)
Prizes:
  • Summer Love Set
    • Summer Love Hat
    • Summer Love Sword
    • 1 or 2x Summer Love Shield
    • variable amounts of Summer Love Accessory
  • 2x Alchemist Nate Card
  • 3x Blacksmith Marx Card
  • variable amounts of Dorothy Card
  • variable amounts of 500 Galder Coupon
Items needed:
  • 1x Dorothy Card
  • 1x Lab Map
  • 1x Lab Key
  • 5x Secret Letter
  • at last 10x R.B. Gunpowder
  • at last 10x Color Powder
Notes: Gunpowder Marble is drilled at everywhere and your contents are (one of them):
  • 100 Galder Coupon
  • 500 Galder Coupon
  • Premium Gunpowder
  • Black Gunpowder
  • R.B. Gunpowder
  • Color Powder

Alchemist Nate - Gloomy Heart

[edit]
NPC: Alchemist Nate Location: Gate of Black Swamp
Quest Cycles: Each 10 Time(s) Condition: Level 5+, have Dorothy Card
Level Request Reward TM
5~25 10x Angry Sand Demon (Lv.15) in 30 min 3x 500 Galder Coupon, Summer Love Accessory 2,372
26~55 10x Angry Mantis (Lv.35) in 30 min 3x 500 Galder Coupon, Summer Love Accessory 86,420
56~90 10x Angry Aposis (Lv.70) in 30 min 3x 500 Galder Coupon, Summer Love Accessory 175,403
91~120 10x Angry Swamp Shark (Lv.105) in 30 min 3x 500 Galder Coupon, Summer Love Accessory 270,398
121+ 10x Angry Isabelle (Lv.140) in 30 min 3x 500 Galder Coupon, Summer Love Accessory 479,978
Found at:
Angry Sand Demon is found at Beach Field 2 - Center and Beach Field 6 - Statue of Anubis.

Angry Mantis is found at Southeast Forest and Southwest Forest.

Angry Aposis is found at Relics Field 1 - Atlas and Relics Field 4 - Altar of Round.

Angry Swamp Shark is found at Swamp Field 1 - Black Hole and Swamp Field 4 - Dark Omen.

Angry Isabelle is found at Rose Field 2 - Maze of Black Rose and Rose Field 3 - Maze of Blue Rose.
Notes: Dorothy Card is obtained by complete Alchemist Nate - Guardian's Worry and Alchemist Nate - Gate of Black Swamp.
