Rose Garden is a maze-like garden with a beautiful design. There is a safe area inside of the maze called the "Secret Garden" and only those who have its key can enter it.


This area with graceful and elegant monsters is perfect for users Lv115 ~ Lv130. There is a magnificent looking castle on the north of the garden, called Rose Garden Dungeon or Vamp Castle.
