  • 54,089 (for Level 15~40)

  • 4,483,869 (for Level 141+)

  • 35,047 (for Level 15~40)

  • 1,806,347 (for Level 71~100)
  • 2,479,727 (for Level 101~140)
  • 3,355,507 (for Level 141+)

  • Halloween Witch Hat
  • Tricky Hairband
  • Treat Pumpkin Hat
  • Halloween Ring
  • Pumpkin Ring
  • Fairy's Fireworks
  • Befana's Memo
  • Eclipse's Memo
  • Toy Map
Items needed:
  • 10x Befana's Cookie
  • 5x Pocket Watch
  • 1x Secret Toy
  • 16x Sparkler
Notes: Halloween Box is drilled at Halloween Field 1 and 2 and dropped by Lost Crow (Lv.15), Lost Tritch (Lv.55), Crazy Model Skeleton (Lv.90), Trouble Mr. Freak (Lv.130), and Trouble Maid Lydia (Lv.180). It has the following contents (one of them):
  • Halloween Certificate
  • Sparkler
  • Halloween Candy
  • Halloween Cookie
  • Halloween Chocolate
  • Halloween Caramel
