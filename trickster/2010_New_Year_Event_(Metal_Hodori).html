Wicked Electric Snail 30 is found at Beach Field 2 - Center and Beach Field 6 - Statue of Anubis.

Wicked Electric Snail 60 is found at Relics Field 1 - Atlas and Relics Field 4 - An Alter for Sacrifice.

Wicked Electric Snail 120 is found at Seabed Field 1 - North and Seabed Field 4 - Undersea Grave.

Wicked Electric Snail 160 is found at Swamp Field 1 - Black Hole and Swamp Field 4 - Dark Omen.

Wicked Electric Snail 230 is found at Snow Field 1 - Merry Christmas and Snow Field 4 - Happy New Year.
