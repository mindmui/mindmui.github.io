How to Obtain 3rd Job Skills

From ggFTW Trickster Wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

From time to time, new skills are added to the game, for all classes. The 2nd Job skills are really easy to get, as you only have to buy them from your Job Master. But it gets a little bit tricky when it comes to 3rd Job Skills (only for Pure Jobs): some of them were initially released along with the whole 3rd Job Advancement process; these ones are obtainable from Card Packs, sold by Card Girl and Merchant Miguel. These packs will not always contain 3rd job skill cards. The contents range from Empty Card to character cards. Each pack contains 3 cards, but each may be a different card. Newly added skills can now be obtained via several ways, be it monster drops, NPC quests, or new Card Packs:


Contents

  • 1 3rd Job Initial Release
  • 2 June 21, 2008 Release
  • 3 January 07, 2009 Release


3rd Job Initial Release

Card Girl and Merchant Miguel sell Card Packs : Feoh, Ur, Thorn, Ansur, and Rad.

  • Champion Skills:
    • Adrenaline is obtained by opening Feoh.
    • Flash Cut is obtained by opening Ur.
  • Gladiator Skills:
    • Sonic Slash is obtained by opening Feoh.
    • Suffocate is obtained by opening Ur.
    • Shadow is obtained by opening Thorn.
  • Soul Master Skills:
    • Aqua Bomb is obtained by opening Feoh.
    • Staff of Thunder is obtained by opening Ur.
    • Dragon Storm is obtained by opening Thorn.
    • Earthquake is obtained by opening Ansur.
    • Raging Storm is obtained by opening Rad.
  • Priest Skills:
    • Med. Cure is obtained by opening Ur.
    • Searing Light is obtained by opening Thorn.
  • Dark Lord Skill:
    • Fear is obtained by opening Thorn.
    • Scythe is obtained by opening Ansur.
    • Staff of Agony is obtained by opening Rad.
  • Thief Master Skills:
    • Hide is obtained by opening Feoh.
    • Sudden Attack is obtained by opening Feoh.
    • Strong Mind is obtained by opening Ur.
    • Explosives Expert is obtained by opening Thorn.
  • Scientist Skills:
    • Revealing Eye is obtained by opening Ansur.
    • Berserk is obtained by opening Ansur.
  • Primadonna Skills:
    • 2 Hit Combo is obtained by opening Rad.
    • Entrancing Wink is obtained by opening Rad.
  • Gambler Skills:
    • Power Charging is obtained by opening Ansur.
    • Full House is obtained by opening Rad.


June 21, 2008 Release

A new Card Pack Ken was introduced, along with several skill quests and monster drops.

  • Champion Skill:
    • Flaming Fist is obtained from completing Boxer Jeanne - Fist of Will.
  • Gladiator Skill:
    • Gale Strike is dropped by Iron Knight (Lv.203).
  • Soul Master Skills:
    • Undine's Blessing is dropped by Icicler (Lv.228).
    • Raion's Blessing is dropped by Bone Magician (Lv.231).
    • Salamander's Blessing is dropped by Aabon (Lv.307).
    • Gnome's Blessing is dropped by Laki (Lv.278).
    • Sylph's Blessing is dropped by Mister Freaks (Lv.191).
  • Priest Skill:
    • Celestial Strike is obtained from completing Magician Louie - Ancient Legacy.
  • Dark Lord Skill:
    • Dark Commando is obtained from completing Fortune Teller - Dark Commando.
  • Thief Master Skill:
    • Poison Pouch is obtained from completing Explorer Reina - Ancient Liquid.
  • Scientist Skill:
    • Gun Overdrive is obtained by opening Ken.
  • Primadonna Skill:
    • Raging Nail is obtained by opening Ken.
  • Gambler Skill:
    • Heavy Hit is obtained by opening Ken.


January 07, 2009 Release

A new Card Pack Wunjo was introduced, along with new skill quests and monster drops.

  • Champion Skill:
    • Mega Flash Cut is obtained by opening Wunjo.
  • Gladiator Skill:
    • Chi Sword is obtained from completing Warrior Kei - Fight With Your Soul!.
  • Soul Master Skills:
    • Undine's Garden is dropped by Legendary Siremaid (Lv.130).
    • Raion's Space is dropped by Electric Snail (Lv.164).
    • Salamander's Territory is dropped by Fire Moth (Lv.289).
    • Gnome's Domain is dropped by Kilimanjaro (Lv.273).
    • Sylph's Playground is dropped by Moss Moth (Lv.48).
  • Priest Skill:
    • Sacred Oath is obtained from completing Magician Louie - Power of Faith.
  • Dark Lord Skill:
    • Ghostly Whisper is obtained by opening Wunjo.
  • Thief Master Skill:
    • Poison Assault is obtained from completing Explorer Reina - Poisonous Crafts.
  • Scientist Skill:
    • Sharp Sense is obtained by opening Wunjo.
  • Primadonna Skill:
    • Sharp Scream is obtained by opening Wunjo.
  • Gambler Skill:
    • (Summon) Card Soldier is obtained from completing Frog Shaman - Card Master Secret.
