Description

Megalo Company wants 2011 to be the best Christmas ever and is revealing a festive Winter Fiesta Party event! Come out and check out Christmas time in the Fiesta Zone! Collect marbles and exchange them for special holiday rewards!


Instructions

  1. A Fiesta Ticket will be distributed to your My Shop Inventory at first login of each day.
  2. Fiesta Zone will open up twice for 20 minutes each time during the event period.
  3. Kill monsters in the Fiesta Zone and monsters will drop Fiesta Marbles at a low rate.
  4. Bring all your Fiesta Marbles to Heidi at the entrance of the Fiesta Waiting Room and exchange them for special rewards.
