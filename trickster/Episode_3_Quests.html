Episode 3
Dreams of an Empire
In the far depths of Mirage Island, the rumored paradise, Alteo Empire can be found. However, unlike the rumors, it looks barren and desolate. There, adventures learn about how Tricksters came about and about the secret behind the demise of Alteo Empire. Also, adventurers meet the protagonist Nefertiti and learn about her destined lover Enkicladus and the enormous power of revenge being born again...
