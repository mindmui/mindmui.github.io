Category:Etc.

From ggFTW Trickster Wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

Etc. Items are items that can be stacked like Use Items, but don't have a direct-use (double-click). However, they do have a wide range of other uses, ranging from quest items to skill mastery items. While others, have no use other than being sold to an NPC. Etc. Items can be obtained from a monster, drilled, compounded, or received from a quest.

Some Etc. Items like Ice can only be obtained during a Event.


Here you find all Etc. Items distributed by alphabetical order. Please, see below.
