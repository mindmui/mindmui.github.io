Special Skills Enlightment Skills

These are the (rare) skills you can obtain from Events.

Note: Keep in mind, when using the formulas, to change the percents to decimals. To do so, move the decimal two places to the left. Ex. 65% = .65, 150% = 1.50

Types of Skills:

  • Timer Skill
    • The skill is applied to the self or others for a given period.
    • This skill type usually consists of skills that change character attributes.
  • On/Off Skill
    • One time use skill.
    • An attack skill or attack magic spell would fall in this category.
  • Passive Skill
    • Once learned, the Passive Skills are in effect continuously without the use of MP or special movement.
    • Learning a special technique or skills that raise stats like MP and weight are Passive Skills.

Formula No formula
Notes You get the ability to gain Stress Points. When the stress reach the top of the gauge you go in crazy drilling mode, where you can drill up items instantly, also having the lucky chance to get a rain of items.
There are two skills building on this skill, namely Anger Management and Mega Crazy Drilling.
Skill card is obtainable through quest: Driller Marky - Driller King's Crazy Skill.
